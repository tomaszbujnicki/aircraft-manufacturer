Foremen

Foremen are important in the production process.
They supervise workers and help them with production.
Each foreman can care for four workers.

If there are too few foremen, workers work less efficiently, up to 50%.

"I'm working on it."

Human Resources

Human resources take care of all employees.
You need people in HR to hire more employees.

If you employ too many other employees, you cannot fire people from HR, first terminate contracts with other employees.

"Beautiful and talkative girls always work in the HR department."

Traders

Traders don't let prices fall too low.
Keeping prices at an optimal level is crucial for the company's development.

Traders care about all prices of aircraft, so it's worth abandoning some projects. Then they will be able to focus only on the ones you produce.

"I can sell wings to a bird."
